1. Site Identity & Purpose

Proposed Site Name

MovieScoop (Movie & TV Recommendation Website)

Reason: A crisp, memorable and simple blending 'Cinema' and 'Suggestion'. It communicates immediate utility to streaming-fatigued users looking for quick, high-quality entertainment matches.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide entertainment recommendations while helping users explore new content and organize their viewing preferences in an easy, interactive, and responsive way[cite: 4]. By fetching metadata records dynamically, it cuts through choice paralysis to deliver tailored recommendations with zero placeholder text.

2. Target Audience Scenarios

This architectural framework builds layouts designed to immediately satisfy these two primary user search workflows:

  • Scenario A: "I want to watch a highly-rated Sci-Fi movie released after 2020 tonight. How can I filter down a massive list of options instantly without reloading the page, read a quick synopsis, and see its rating?"[cite: 4]
  • Scenario B: "I frequently browse recommendations on my phone during my commute. How can I save three interesting TV shows to a personal watchlist so that they remain safely saved even if I close my mobile browser?"[cite: 4]

3. Visual Design System

Color Palette (Movie Theater Theme)

#0F172A Primary Slate (Background)
#F5BB00 Accent Amber (CTAs/Buttons)
#F8FAFC Light Neutral (High-Contrast Text)
#1E293B Card Surface (Component Fill)

Typography Specification

Heading Elements: "Play", sans-serif

Body Prose Elements: "Roboto", sans-serif

Rationale: A bold, geometric heading typeface brings an immersive, premium streaming-platform vibe, while a highly legible neutral body font makes reading dense movie summaries effortless.
