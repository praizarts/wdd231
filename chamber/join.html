Non-Profit Membership Benefits

  • Eligibility: Exclusive to 501(c)(3) organizations.
  • Basic Listing: Inclusion in our digital community directory.
  • Event Access: Invitation to monthly community mixers.
  • Newsletter: Subscription to the "Chamber Pulse" weekly update.

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • General Membership: Open to all small businesses and startups.
  • Enhanced Listing: Directory listing with your business logo and social media links.
  • Spotlight Feature: One "New Member Spotlight" post on our official Facebook page.
  • Training: Access to "Business Basics" webinar recordings.
  • Ribbon Cutting: Complimentary grand opening or anniversary ceremony support.

Silver Membership Benefits

  • All Bronze Benefits included.
  • Training Workshops: Free access for up to 3 employees.
  • Spotlight Feature: One dedicated social media post per quarter.
  • Directory Upgrade: Priority placement in the digital member directory.
  • Event Discounts: 10% discount on all Chamber banquet tickets.

Gold Membership Benefits

  • All Silver Benefits included.
  • Homepage Spotlight: Permanent logo placement on the Chamber homepage.
  • VIP Access: 2 complimentary tickets to the Annual Gala.
  • Keynote Opportunities: First priority for speaking slots at networking luncheons.
  • Ad Credits: ₦175,000 annual credit toward Chamber digital advertising.
  • Board Eligibility: Exclusive eligibility to run for the Chamber Board of Directors.
